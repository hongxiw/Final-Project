Welcome to Two Layer Tic Tac Toe

Rules/Instructions:

  • - You will need to occupy a space twice to fully take it and prevent the opponent from choosing it
  • - You do not need fully captured spots to win. Three in a row with your letter, even half-captured, can win
  • - You can take a half-captured spot away from the opponent, as long as the opponent did not just pick that spot
  • - X always moves first
